Desele's Debt

Quest GiverEydis Fire-Eye in Balmora
Prerequisite QuestThe Code Book
Reward100 gold, +5 (Fighters Guild)

Eydis Fire-Eye in Balmora, Guild of Fighters asks you to collect 200 gold of debt money from Helviane Desele, who runs the Suran, Desele's House of Earthly Delights (northeast of Vivec). She apparently owes a client of the Guild, Manos Othreleth on the Dren Plantation.

Helviane is found behind the counter at Desele's House of Earthly Delights. She will pay once you raise her disposition to 75+. Otherwise, kill her and loot the money from her corpse.

If you talk to Percius Mercius in Ald'ruhn about this quest, he has doubts whether this contract is legitimate or not and suggests that you pay the debt money yourself.

You can kill Manos Othreleth without repercussions, sell his armor, and then follow Percius Mercius' advice to pay the debt money yourself with what you've earned from selling the armor, without actually having to spend any of your own money.
