The Pudai Eggmine

Quest GiverHrundi in Sadrith Mora
Prerequisite QuestEngaer's Bounty
Reward10000 gold, +5 (Fighters Guild)

Hrundi in Sadrith Mora, Wolverine Hall: Fighter's Guild asks you recover 7x Golden Egg from the legendary Pudai Egg Mine. The mine is believed to be somewhere on or near the island of Sheogorad (the large island in the north area of the map) but that's all the information he has.

The Pudai Egg Mine is on the main island of the Sheogorad region. It is to the far west of Mzuleft, past the inlet. Mzuleft is to the southwest of Dagon Fel. No one on the island knows where the mine is, so there's no use asking around.

To find the Pudai Egg Mine, leave the guild and take a boat to Dagon Fel. Exit Dagon Fel to the west. Go south when the path splits. At the next split, go west (passing an Orc named Bulak gra-Murug). Continue west past a few more forks in the road, and the mine will be on the right. If the path ends in water, turn around and walk back east and look to the left; the mine will be very close.
