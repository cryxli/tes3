Bandits in Suran

Quest GiverPercius Mercius in Ald-ruhn
Prerequisite QuestBeneran's Bounty
Reward1000 gold, +5 (Fighters Guild), +5 (Hlaalu)

Percius Mercius in the Ald-ruhn, Guild of Fighters asks you go to Serjo Avon Oran in Suran and help take care of some bandits for him.

Avon Oran can be found in Suran, Oran Manor in the south side of Suran. He tells you that the bandits have been attacking from the Saturan caverns, northeast of the town on the other side of the hills. He asks you to kill their leader, Daldur Sarys, and report back to him when you have done so.
