The Verethi Gang

Quest GiverEydis Fire-Eye in Balmora
Prerequisite QuestAlof and the Orcs
Reward1000 gold, +10 (Fighters Guild)

Eydis Fire-Eye in Balmora, Guild of Fighters ask you to kill the leader of the Verethi Gang, Dovres Verethi.

The Gang are smugglers based in Mannammu, just a little southeast of Pelagiad. Though you only have to kill Dovres Verethi, you'll have to go through a half-dozen of his friends to get to him at the very end of the caves. You can skip a couple of his goons, since there is a way directly to him via an aquatic passage. Alternatively, you can simply sneak past all of them using stealth or magic.
