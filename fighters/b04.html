The Code Book

Quest GiverEydis Fire-Eye in Balmora
Prerequisite QuestThe Telvanni Agents
Reward50 gold, +5 (Fighters Guild), -10 (Thieves Guild)

Eydis Fire-Eye in Balmora, Guild of Fighters asks you to retrieve a code book currently held by Sottilde at the Balmora, South Wall Cornerclub. If you check with Percius Mercius he'll tell you that the mission seems okay but that you should try to avoid killing Sottilde. He calls her a "pushover" and suggests you talk her into handing it over.

If you are not a member of the Thieves Guild you can raise her disposition to 70 or more to get bk_sottildescodebook. If you are a member of the guild, you have to pick her pocket. Taunting and subsequently killing her is also an option.
