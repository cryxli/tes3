Flin for Elith-Pal

Quest GiverPercius Mercius in Ald-ruhn
Prerequisite QuestBandits in Suran
Reward500 gold, Cyrodiilic Brandy, +5 (Fighters Guild)

Percius Mercius in the Ald-ruhn, Guild of Fighters asks you to deliver a load of twenty Flin to Dangor in the Elith-Pal Mine on the base of the Red Mountain just west of the Zainab Camp (ask him about the camp to have it marked on your map).

The quickest route is to take a Silt Strider up to Khuul, a ship to Dagon Fel, then on to Tel Mora and Vos before trekking southwest to the mine (it is indeed directly west of the Zainab Camp in the mountains). Using the Zainab Camp as a reference point, take the path north away from camp and it will immediately turn west. Follow the path toward Red Mountain and you will come across an entrance to the range, flanked by rock spires. Go straight down this valley and you will see the mine on the left.
