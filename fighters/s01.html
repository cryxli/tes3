Battle at Nchurdamz

Quest GiverHrundi in Sadrith Mora
Prerequisite Questjoin the Fighters Guild
Reward500 gold, +5 (Fighters Guild)

Hrundi in Sadrith Mora, Wolverine Hall: Fighter's Guild asks you to travel to the Dwemer ruins of Nchurdamz to help Larienna Macrina explore the ruins in search of a great beast (the position should be marked on your map, far south of Sadrith Mora).

Travel to Nchurdamz and talk to Larienna Macrina, and she'll tell you she is hunting a wounded Daedroth named Hrelvesuu. The ruins are small and filled with a variety of robots to take care of. If you make it to the lava room, you should find a key to the locked (60) and trapped doors to the west. Behind the doors you should find the Daedroth that must be killed to fulfill the contract.
