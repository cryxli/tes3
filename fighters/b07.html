Alof and the Orcs

Quest GiverEydis Fire-Eye in Balmora
Prerequisite QuestGra-Bol's Bounty
Reward500 gold, +10 (Fighters Guild)

Eydis Fire-Eye in Balmora, Guild of Fighters ask you to investigate some Orcs at a Daedric ruin for the Duke Vedam Dren himself. And that you should talk to Alof the Easterner, a local farmer for more information.

Alof's Farmhouse is northeast of Pelagiad and north of the Arvel Plantation (a small hut on the north shore of Lake Amaya), just east of the Fields of Kummu. Alof tells you that the Orcs come from Ashunartes, a Daedric ruin northeast of there. He says that if you kill their leader, Burub gra-Bamog, the rest of the Orcs should leave the farmers in peace.
