Remove the Heads of the Thieves Guild

Quest GiverSjoring Hard-Heart in Vivec
Prerequisite Questrank of Guardian
Rewardpromotion to Champion, +15 (Fighters Guild)

Once you reach the rank of Guardian, Sjoring Hard-Heart in the Vivec, Guild of Fighters will ask you to kill the three Thieves Guild bosses:

  • Big Helende in the Sadrith Mora, Dirty Muriel's Cornerclub
  • Sugar-Lips Habasi in the Balmora, South Wall Cornerclub
  • Aengoth the Jeweler in Ald-ruhn, The Rat In The Pot

He even forwards you 5,000 gold to cover any expenses during your murder spree.
