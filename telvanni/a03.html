Wizard Spells

Quest GiverMaster Aryon in Tel Vos
Prerequisite QuestMudan-Mul Egg Mine
RewardThe Art of War Magic, +5 (Telvanni)

Master Aryon (Tel Vos, Aryon's Chambers) wants to ensure that a member of House Telvanni is at least competent in the basics of magic. Therefore, he want you to learn three spells:

  • Recall
  • Fireball, Greater Fireball, or Fire Storm
  • Levitate, Great Levitate, or Wild Levitate

He mentions Fevyn Ralen in Vivec and Felara Andrethi in Tel Aruhn as possible teachers.

Return to Master Aryon once you know the spells.
