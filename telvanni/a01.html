Talk to Master Aryon in Tel Vos for chores (Tel Vos, Aryon's Chambers). He wants you to convince Baladas Demnevanni to join the Council.

Go to Gnisis, Arvs-Drelen and talk to Baladas Demnevanni. He agrees to Aryon's proposition, if you first get him three Dwemer Books.
