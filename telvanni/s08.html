Staff of the Silver Dawn

Quest GiverArara Uvulas in Sadrith Mora
Prerequisite QuestSload Soap
RewardGlass Jinkblade, Blind spell, +5 (Telvanni)

Arara Uvulas in the Sadrith Mora, Telvanni Council House wants you to find the Staff of the Silver Dawn.

Go to the Mage's Guild in Wolverine Hall (Sadrith Mora, Wolverine Hall: Mages Guild) to find out who has the silver staff. Convince Arielle Phiencel to sell the staff to you.

Return to Arara to complete the quest.
