Mission to Nchuleft

Quest GiverMallam Ryon in Sadrith Mora
Prerequisite Quest Three Questions for Baladas Demnevanni
RewardGothren's Cephalopod Helm, +5 (Telvanni)

Mallam Ryon in the Sadrith Mora, Telvanni Council House wants you to retrieve some schematics from the Dwemer ruin Nchuleft.

Nchuleft is located north-northwest of the Zainab Camp and west of Vos. The Dwemer Scarab Schematics are on the bottom shelf on the bookcase in the first room to your left. Don't forget to check the ruins for any loot.

Return the schematics to Mallam Ryon.
