Amulet of Unity

Quest GiverEdd Theman in Sadrith Mora
Prerequisite QuestRing of Equity
RewardAmulet of Unity, +5 (Telvanni), +1 Reputation

Edd Theman in the Sadrith Mora, Telvanni Council House has another idea for a suicide mission for himself.

If you get him 5 Standard Potion of Invisibility, he'll try to bring you the Amulet of Unity.

Wait 5 days, and when your journal updates, talk to Eddie to finish the quest.
