Coded Message

Quest GiverGalos Mathendis in Sadrith Mora
Prerequisite Questjoin House Telvanni
Reward500 gold, +5 (Telvanni)

Galos Mathendis in the Sadrith Mora, Telvanni Council House sends you to Tel Fyr to deliver a coded message to Divayth Fyr.

Divayth Fyr can be found in his tower, Tel Fyr, in the islands southwest of Sadrith Mora (Tower of Tel Fyr, Hall of Fyr). Simply deliver the message to the wizard at the top of his tower and receive his response.

Return the response to Galos Mathendis.
