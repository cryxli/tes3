Stronghold - Step 3

Quest GiverLlunela Hleran in Sadrith Mora
Prerequisite Questsecond stage of stronghold
RewardTel Uvirith, Tower Upper +2 Reputation

Talk to Llunela Hleran (Sadrith Mora, Telvanni Council House, Hermitage) again for the finishing touches of your stronghold.

She wants you to retrieve the book Secrets of Dwemer Animunculi, found only in the vampire lair Galom Daeus (Galom Daeus, Observatory), to create the guards.

Return to Llunela with the book to start the construction. The third phase should be finished after 5 days.
