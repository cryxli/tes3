Ring of Equity

Quest GiverEdd Theman in Sadrith Mora
Prerequisite QuestRecruit a Mouth
RewardRing of Equity, +5 (Telvanni), +1 Reputation

After you promote Edd Theman to your Mouth, he will be in the Council House in Sadrith Mora (Sadrith Mora, Telvanni Council House) and wait for you to come up with chores for him to perform.

If you can get him the Key to Tel Naga, he thinks, he can bring you the Ring of Equity.

Wait for 5 days, for your journal to update, to complete the quest.
