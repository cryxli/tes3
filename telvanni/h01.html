Stronghold - Step 1

Quest GiverLlunela Hleran in Sadrith Mora
Prerequisite Questrank of Mouth
Rewardnone

Once you become Aryon's Mouth, you can start the process of building your own stringhold.

To do so, travel to Sadrith Mora and talk to Llunela Hleran in the hermitage of the Telvanni Council House (Sadrith Mora, Telvanni Council House, Hermitage).

She wants you to receive the Construction Contract from Duke Vedam Dren and capture two strong sould.

Travel to Vedam Dren in Ebonheart, Grand Council Chambers and talk to him. After you have sworn an oath to aid the people of Vvardenfell he'll give you the contract.

Easiest way to capture two strong souls is to summon one of the suggested daedra (Winged Twilight, Storm Atronach or Golden Saint).

player->addSoulGem "golden saint" Misc_SoulGem_Grand
She will gladly accept Azura's Star as one of the soul gems, and she will not give it back. Make sure you store it somewhere before you return to her with the filled soul gems.

Return to the full soul gems and the contract to Llunela Hleran.

After 5 days your journal informs you to talk to Llunela Hleran again.

She wants a progress report fromt he foreman at Uvirith's Grave. Go there and talk to Gashnakh gra-Mughol. Return to Llunela Hleran with the update.

Another 5 days later the first stage of the mushroom is complete. Wait for your journal to update.
