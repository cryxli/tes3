Slave Rebellion

Quest GiverFelisa Ulessen in Sadrith Mora
Prerequisite QuestNew Clothes
RewardFirestorm and/or Toxic Cloud Rings or Command spells +5 (Telvanni), +1 Reputation

Felisa Ulessen in Sadrith Mora, Telvanni Council House wants you to end the slave rebellion in the Abebaal Egg Mine.

All the slaves in the mine are on strike. Their leader, Eleedal-Lei, argues for their freedom. You can either kill Eleedal-Lei or bring him the slave key key_abebaalslaves_01 to free all the slaves. The key can be found on a crate next to the kwama queen in the mine.

Return to Felisa Ulessen to complete the quest.
