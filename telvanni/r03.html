Drake's Pride

Quest GiverNeloth in Sadrith Mora
Reward10 gold or the Robe of the Drake's Pride, +5 (Telvanni)

Master Neloth of Tel Naga (Sadrith Mora, Tel Naga Upper Tower) wants the Robe of the Drake's Pride for himself. It is currently worn by Senise Thindo a retainer of Master Gothren of Tel Aruhn.

Travel to Tel Aruhn and talk to people to learn that Thindo is in the tower's living quarters (Tel Aruhn, Tower Living Quarters). Kill Thindo to get the robe.

Return the robe to Neloth to complete the quest.
