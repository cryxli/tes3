Shishi

Quest GiverMaster Aryon in Tel Vos
Prerequisite QuestMages Guild Monopoly
RewardThe Lunar Lorkhan, A Hypothetical Treachery, Silver Staff of War, +5 (Telvanni), +1 Reputation

Another Telvanni Faves Andas of Shishi is under attack. This time by House Redoran. Master Aryon (Tel Vos, Aryon's Chambers) ask you for help.

The tower Shishi is located northwest of Maar Gan. Upon entry to the base, be sure to kill the Redoran force. At first glance, Faves Andas is nowhere to be found. A careful search, however, reveals him in a secret room accessed by touching the Breton skull on the table on the upper floor. Talk with Faves Andas who will ask you to kill the Redoran leader, Brerama Selas, if you haven't already done so.

Return to Master Aryon to complete the quest.
