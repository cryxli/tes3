Kill Banden Indarys

Quest GiverMaster Aryon in Tel Vos
Prerequisite QuestKill Raynasa Rethan
Reward+10 (Telvanni), +1 Reputation

Master Aryon (Tel Vos, Aryon's Chambers) will ask you to "retire" Banden Indarys, master of the Redoran stronghold Indarys Manor.

Indarys Manor is in Bal Isra, along the road northwest of Ald'ruhn and southwest of Maar Gan. Just head out to the manor. Once you arrive, the Redoran guards will make it clear that you aren't welcome, but none of them will take the step of attacking you. Enter the main house of the manor and travel down into the bedroom. Your target will be inside. He is automatically hostile towards you. Don't worry about watching your back, as none of the other residents will join the fight unless you attack them.
