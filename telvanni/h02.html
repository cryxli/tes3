Stronghold - Step 2

Quest GiverLlunela Hleran in Sadrith Mora
Prerequisite Questfirst stage of stronghold
RewardTel Uvirith, Tower Lower

Once the first phase of construction on your stronghold is complete, you can speak with Llunela Hleran (Sadrith Mora, Telvanni Council House, Hermitage) again to begin your first upgrade.

She will ask you for 5000 gold Gold_001 and some misc_dwrv_artifact70 from the ruins of Mzanchend (northwest of your stronghold, Mzanchend). Bring the schematics and the gold back to Hleran to start the upgrade construction.

5 days later you will receive a journal entry once construction is complete. The second phase results in two homes and the lower tower.
