Flesh Made Whole

Quest GiverDratha in Tel Mora
RewardAmulet of Spell Absorption, (4 Scrolls of Summon Golden Saint), +5 (Telvanni)

Master Dratha in the Upper Tower in Tel Mora (Tel Mora, Upper Tower) will ask you to retrieve the Amulet of Flesh Made Whole, which is currently in the possession of Neloth's captain archer in Tel Naga in Sadrith Mora.

Travel to Sadrith Mora and talk to the guards in Tel Naga to learn that the captain's name is Berengeval. If you persuade them sufficiently (disposition 60+) you'll also learn that he is in the tower's general quarters (Sadrith Mora, Tel Naga General Quarters). You'll have to find and kill Berengeval to get the amulet.

Return the amulet to Master Dratha to complete the quest.
