House Telvanni

To join House Telvanni, travel to Sadrith Mora and speak with the Mouths in the Telvanni Council Hall (Sadrith Mora, Telvanni Council House). If you have Tamriel Rebuilt installed, you can also join by speaking with Vero Renim in the Port Telvannis, Telvanni Council House: Entrance.

If you are expelled, you can make amends with anyone ranked Mouth or above, except Therana. You can rejoin as many times as needed.

Note that you do not actually have to satisfy the requirements for Magister or Archmagister. As soon as you meet the requirements for Master, you will also be given the option to advance to Magister (which begins the quest Archmagister Gothren, automatically advancing you to Archmagister on completion.)

If you have Tamriel Rebuilt installed, the faction reputation for advancement is heigher than shown here. You'll have to do some of the quests of the Telvanni Mouths in Port Telvannis, too (Port Telvannis, Telvanni Council House: Chambers).
