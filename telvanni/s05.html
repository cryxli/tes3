Three Questions

Quest GiverMallam Ryon in Sadrith Mora
Prerequisite Questjoin House Telvanni
Reward3x Messenger Scroll, +5 (Telvanni)

Mallam Ryon in the Sadrith Mora, Telvanni Council House sends you to Gnisis to ask Baladas Demnevanni three questions on behalf of this Master Gothren.

  • Disappearances of the dwemer
  • Dwemer artifacts
  • Dwemer language

Travel to Gnisis and enter Baladas Demnevanni's Velothi tower (Gnisis, Arvs-Drelen) and ask him the three questsion.

When you speak with Baladas, the answers he'll give you will depend on his disposition towards you. If he doesn't like you, you may not even get an answer at all (though you can still complete the quest since you only need to ask the questions). If his disposition is higher than 80, you should get an informative answer about each of the three.

Return to Mallam Ryon to complete the quest.
