Sload Soap

Quest GiverArara Uvulas in Sadrith Mora
Prerequisite Questjoin House Telvanni
Reward500 gold, +5 (Telvanni)

Arara Uvulas in the Sadrith Mora, Telvanni Council House will ask you to retrieve 5 pieces of Sload Soap.

Head over to Anis Seloth's shop in town (Sadrith Mora, Anis Seloth: Alchemist) to buy the soap, as they are not readily available in the wild.

Give Arara the soap.
