Black Jinx

Quest GiverRaven Omayn in Sadrith Mora
Prerequisite Questjoin House Telvanni
RewardDemoralize Humanoid and Creature spells, +5 (Telvanni)

When Raven Omayn in the Sadrith Mora, Telvanni Council House mentions the Black Jinx, he is most likely not too serious that you can obtain it.

Ask around town to learn that the Black Jinx is in the hands of the Morag Tong. In the Morag Tong guild hall in town (Sadrith Mora, Morag Tong Guild) speak with Alven Salas. Unless you are a high ranking member of the Morag Tong yourself, you have to kill him, knock him out or defeat him in a duel.

Return to Raven Omayn with the ring to complete the quest.
