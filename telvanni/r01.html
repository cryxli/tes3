Dwemer Books

Quest GiverBaladas Demnevanni in Arvs-Drelen
RewardOndusi's Key, Amulet of Admonition, Second Barrier Belt, Surefeet, +5 (Telvanni)

When asked for chores Baladas Demnevanni in Gnisis, Arvs-Drelen wants you to get him three Dwemer related books:

  • Nchunak's Fire and Faith
  • Chronicles of Nchuleft
  • Antecedants of Dwemer Law

If you have the money, the easiest method is to simply buy the books from Dorisa Darvel in Balmora (Balmora, Dorisa Darvel: Bookseller, Chronicles of Nchuleft and Nchunak's Fire and Faith), and Jobasha in the Foreign Quarter in Vivec (Vivce, Jobasha's Rare Books, Chronicles of Nchuleft and Antecedants of Dwemer Law). Or, you can steal them from Vivec, Hall of Justice Secret Library>, and, the Holamayan Monastery.

Return to Baladas Demnevanni with the books to complete the quest.
