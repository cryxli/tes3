Dahrk Mezalf

Quest GiverBaladas Demnevanni in Arvs-Drelen
Prerequisite Questrank of Mouth and Dwemer Books
RewardDwemer Shock Centurion Guard, Ring of Lightning Storm, +10 (Telvanni), +1 Reputation

Once you reach the rank of Mouth, Baladas Demnevanni in Gnisis, Arvs-Drelen will ask you to find an ancient summoning ring belonging to Dahrk Mezalf. Baladas believes that the ring can be found in the Dwemer village of Bthungthumz.

Bthungthumz is located northwest of Maar Gan and southwest of the Urshilaku Camp. The ruins inside are quite small and you should quickly find Mezalf's spirit haunting the place.

Return to Baladas Demnevanni with the ring for a reward.
