Muck

Quest GiverRaven Omayn in Sadrith Mora
Prerequisite QuestMuck
RewardCure Common Disease spell, +5 (Telvanni)

Raven Omayn in the Sadrith Mora, Telvanni Council House wants you to fetch him 5 units of Muck.

The surest route is to buy the muck from Anis Seloth's Alchemy shop in town (Sadrith Mora, Anis Seloth: Alchemist).
