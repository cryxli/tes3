Odirniran

Quest GiverMaster Aryon in Tel Vos
Prerequisite QuestWizard Spells
Reward7x Milyn Faram's Scroll, Silver Staff of Hunger, Incident in Necrom, +5 (Telvanni), +1 Reputation

The Telvanni stronhold Odirniran is invaded by House Hlaalu and Master Aryon (Tel Vos, Aryon's Chambers) wants you to intervene.

Milyn Faram is the master of Odirniran that is located east of Molag Mar and south of the Dwemer ruins of Nchurdamz.

Kill the Hlaalu forces, especially their leader Remasa Othril, but don't hurt Vedelea Othril! Then talk to Milyn Faram.

Return to Master Aryon to complete the quest.
