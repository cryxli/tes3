Daedra Skin

Quest GiverGalos Mathendis in Sadrith Mora
Prerequisite QuestCure Blight
Reward300 gold, Paralysis Spell, +5 (Telvanni)

Galos Mathendis in the Sadrith Mora, Telvanni Council House will ask you to find some Daedra Skin to deliver to Master Aryon in Tel Vos.

Visit Anis Seloth's shop in town (Sadrith Mora, Anis Seloth: Alchemist) and buy the skin.

Deliverying it to Master Aryon in his chambers in Tel Vos (Tel Vos, Aryon's Chambers) will end the quest.
