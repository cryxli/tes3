Cure Blight

Quest GiverGalos Mathendis in Sadrith Mora
Prerequisite QuestCoded Message
Reward500 gold, +5 (Telvanni)

Galos Mathendis in the Sadrith Mora, Telvanni Council House has another delivery for you. Obtain and delivery 3 potions of Cure Blight Disease to Andil in the Services Tower of Tel Vos (Tel Vos, Services Tower).

Visit Pierlette Rostorard in town (Sadrith Mora, Pierlette Rostorard: Apothecary) to fetch the potions. If you raise her Disposition above 90, she will give them to you for free.

Once you have the potions, take a boat to Tel Mora and then to Vos. You'll find Andil at his shop in the Services Tower. Return to Galos Mathendis once you've delivered the potions to complete the quest and receive 500 gold.
