Easy Alchemy

The successful, future alchemy master needs the right tools. They are freely available in the Caldery mages guild (Caldera, Guild of Mages). A complete master level alchemy set can be found on top of the tower. They count as stolen, so don't get caught by any guards, or they'll take them from you.

In the chapel of Vos (Vos, Vos Chapel) Eldrilu Dalen sells alchemy ingredients. Reopening her barter screen will restock items for infinite bartering.

  • Brew fortify intelligence potions from Ash Yam and Netch Leather.
  • Brew fortify luck potions from Corkbulb Root and Guar Hide.
  • The effects of the resulting potions stack.
  • Both effects directly affect the outcome of the alchemy process.

Therefore, drink potions to boost intelligence and luck to brew more potent potions. Occationally sell some potions to buy more ingredients. It does not take long for the potions to become so powerful that no traders can afford them anymore.
