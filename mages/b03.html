Four Types of Flowers

Quest GiverAjira in Balmora
Prerequisite QuestFake Soul Gem
Reward6x Cheap Restore Magicka, +5 (Mages Guild)

Chat with Ajira in the Balmora, Guild of Mages about duties, and she'll send you on an errand much like her previous mushroom quest. Ajira asks you to collect four different local flower specimens:

  • Gold Kanet
  • Stoneflower Petals
  • Willow Anther
  • Heather

All of these can be found on the shores of Lake Amaya, which is southeast of Balmora.

You can actually buy Willow Anther and Heather from Ajira herself. You can buy Stoneflower Petals from Llathyno Hlaalu in the basement of Balmora Temple. You can buy Gold Kanet from Peragon at nearby Moonmoth Legion Fort.
