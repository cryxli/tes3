A Potion from Skink-in-Tree's-Shade

Quest GiverEdwinna Elbert in Ald-ruhn
Prerequisite QuestChronicles of Nchuleft
RewardExclusive Potion of Shadow, +5 (Mages Guild)

It seems that Edwinna Elbert in the Ald-ruhn, Guild of Mages is waiting for a Potion of Detect Creatures from Skink-in-Tree's-Shade in the Sadrith Mora, Wolverine Hall: Mage's Guild. Edwinna needs you to go to Sadrith Mora and check on Skink-in-Tree's-Shade's progress for her.

Take the guild guide to Sadrith Mora and chat with Skink-in-Tree's-Shade about 'Detect Creatures Potion' and Skink will gladly retrieve it for you. Return it to Edwinna Elbert.
