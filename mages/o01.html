Restocking Tamriel Rebuilt

Amelphia Tarramon in Old Ebonheart, Guild of Mages: Basement is running low on ingredients and wants you to replenish them.

  • 2x Ornada Egg
  • 1x Hamum Root
  • 1x Amethyst

You can get the Hamum root from Kassad in his shop (Old Ebonheart, Kassad: Herbalist). Ornada Eggs can be obtained from Pinefist Foods (Old Ebonheart, Pinefist Foods), and Amethysts from the Fetcher's Pawn (Old Ebonheart, The Fetcher's Pawn).
