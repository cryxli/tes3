RewardSpell: Auridia Gematria (Fortify Skill Conjuration 15-30 pts for 60 secs on Self), +5 (Mages Guild)

Banvira Auctoria in the Firewatch, Guild of Mages informs you that Gindaman has a task for you. To pass the Geth Sigil Amulet of Entry to pass the magical barrier to his quarters.

Enter Gindaman's quarters and speak to him about duties while at the rank of Warlock or above. Gindaman will ask you to retrieve the Scroll of Forbidden Domination from an enchantress named Ostrala Rouillac, who resides within the Velothi Mountains (Hasarund). He'll suggest that you pick up her trail by speaking to a scout, Derana Galvith, in the mountain settlement of Uman (Uman, Derana Galvith's House).

From Firewatch to Uman is quite a journey. Guild guide to Helnim, then a boat to Darvonis, and to Old Ebonheart, guild guide to Andothren, followed by a silt strider to Omaynis, then Bodrum. The paved road leaving Bodrum in a roughly southwesterly direction is the one to follow. It'll eventually wind down a steep slope into the Armun Ashlands, passed the Dwemer ruin of Mvelthngth-Schel. Continue following the cobbled section, over a rockslide, and deep into the Ashlands until you find a signposted junction to Uman. From there, follow the signs until you reach the sleepy Redoran village.

Ostrala Rouillac is willing to part with the scroll, if you bring her the soul of a goblin warchief, Urak, found in the vast cavern system of Kharom (Kharom, Forges). Or, you can kill her.
