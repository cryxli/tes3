Another necramancer thread needs dealing with, sais Banvira Auctoria in the Firewatch, Guild of Mages.

Shalath Abkala resides in the caverns under the volcano, Mount Sarrunabi (Mount Sarrunabi, Cavern). Prepare yourself for extensive combat before setting out from Firewatch as Shalath Abkala has amassed quite the army of former legionaries.

Mount Sarrunabi lies close by, to the north of the city. To enter Shalath's den, exit the southern gate of the city and follow the road, taking the first fork to the left. You will find yourself opposite the entrance to the Mount Sarrunabi Cavern - an extensive subterranean landscape.
