Delivery for Dagmund Tamriel Rebuilt

Quest GiverHalan Macrinus in Firewatch
Prerequisite Questjoin the Mages Guild
Reward2x Quality Restore Magicka or a Journeyman's Alembic, +5 (Mages Guild)

Speak to Halan Macrinus in the Firewatch, Guild of Mages to learn that the chapter needs two samples of a rare flower called the Lady's Mantle Lady's Mantle Leaves. Since the plant does not grow in Morrowind, Marcel Artaud recommends taking some from the personal chambers of the Duchess of Firewatch.

Go to the Firewatch, Ember Keep: Great Hall to ask Duchess Perulia Jandacia for permission (Disposition 15+). You can skip asking, but then need 90+ Disposition with Edwinn Moliere (Firewatch, Ember Keep: Ducal Chambers) to take the samples. Or, you can simply steal the leaves.

Bring the leaves to Marcel Artaud then talk to Halan Macrinus of your reward.
