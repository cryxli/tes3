Delivery for Dagmund Tamriel Rebuilt

Quest GiverHalan Macrinus in Firewatch
Quest EndBanvira Auctoria in Firewatch
Prerequisite QuestLady's Mantle
Reward50 gold, 2x Scroll of Windform

Halan Macrinus in the Firewatch, Guild of Mages needs you to deliver some potions to Dagmund Wind-Whisker in Nivalis, Icebreaker Keep: Mages Guild Outpost.

When handing over the potions Dagmund Wind-Whisker asks you to check in on his apprentice Claudine Hesault. She has not returned for her lessons in several days.

Find her in her home in Nevalis, Claudine Hesault's House. Look up! She is stuck under the roof. Cast a dispel other spell on her. You can purchase a Dispel Other spell from Ainssa in the Firewatch Guild of Mages.

Return to Dagmund Wind-Whisker. He has a report for Banvira Auctoria in Firewatch.
