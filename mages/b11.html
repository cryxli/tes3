Escort Itermerel

Ranis Athrys in the Balmora, Guild of Mages wants Itermerel's research notes. And she is not picky on how you achieve this.

You can steal them, kill Itermerel and loot them from his body, or, as he requests, escort him to Pelagiad and receive them as a thank-you.

You find Itermerel in the Balmora, Eight Plates tavern. Leave Balmora past the silt strider, pass Fort Moonmoth and follow the sign posts until you reach Pelagiad, Halfway Tavern via the main street. Once inside the tavern Itermerel will thank you and you can ask him for the notes.

Return Itermerel's Notes to Ranis Athrys.
