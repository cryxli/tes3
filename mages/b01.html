Four Types of Mushrooms

Quest GiverAjira in Balmora
Prerequisite Questjoin the Mages Guild
Reward4x Cheap Restore Health, +5 (Mages Guild)

Ajira in Balmora, Guild of Mages asks you to collect four different types of mushrooms for her:

  • Luminous Russula
  • Violet Coprinus
  • Bungler's Bane
  • Hypha Facia

Just wander around the swamps across the hills southwest of Balmora and you can collect samples of the four mushrooms very quickly.

If you don't feel like traveling around the countryside in search of mushrooms, you can stop by the Temple in Balmora to buy Bungler's Bane and Hypha Facia from Dralval Andrano, and you can travel to Caldera Guild of Mages using the guild guide to buy Luminous Russula and Violet Coprinus from Ernand Thierry.
