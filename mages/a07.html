Nchuleftingth Expedition

Quest GiverEdwinna Elbert in Ald-ruhn
Prerequisite QuestDwemer Tube from Arkngthunch-Sturdumz
Reward+5 (Mages Guild)

Edwinna Elbert in the Ald-ruhn, Guild of Mages wants you to retrieve the excavation report from their expedition to Nchuleftingth.

Nchuleftingth ruins are northeast of Suran, which is northeast of Vivec. Once you arrive at the ruins Senilias Cadiusus and his daughter are quickly found just inside. It appears they have been having problems and their guide, Anes Vendu, is missing.

Enter the Test of Pattern section to find three cranks along the northeastern wall. Activating the last crank down the hall, will open a secret wall to the lower levels. Folloing the right wall you'll find the corpse of Anes Vendu at the bottom of the Lower Level, next to a disabled Dwemer robot and the book Hanging Gardens of Wasten Coridale. Pick up the book, loot Anes' body for his report, and return to Senilias Cadiusus, who will direct you to take the report back to Edwinna.
