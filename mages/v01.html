Mystery of the Dwarves

Quest GiverTrebonius Artorius in Vivec
Prerequisite Questjoin the Mages Guild
Reward+20 (Mages Guild), +1 Reputation

Talk with Trebonius Artorius in the Vivec, Guild of Mages about duties. He sends you on a wild goose chase to retrieve and translate three books:

  • bk_hanginggardenswasten found in Nchuleftingth
  • bk_EggOfTime found in Mzuleft
  • bk_DivineMetaphysics found in Bethamez

There are two Dwemer experts Baladas Demnevanni in Gnisis, Arvs-Drelen and the last living Dwemer, Yagrum Bagarn in Corprusarium Bowels in Tel Fyr that can translate the old scripture.

Trebonius Artorius will be shocked that someone actually completed one of his missions, and he'll try to play it off like he's not in way over his head.
