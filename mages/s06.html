Quest GiverSkink-in-Tree's-Shade in Sadrith Mora

Skink-in-Tree's-Shade in the Sadrith Mora, Wolverine Hall: Mage's Guild wants you to find Galur Rithari's Papers.

Galur Rithari was a Buoyant Armiger that conducted vampirism and allegedly found a way to cure it.

The notes are quite rare and well guarded:

  • Vivec, Hall of Justice Secret Library
  • Tureynulal, Kagrenac's Library
  • Galom Daeus, Observatory
