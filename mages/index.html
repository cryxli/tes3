Guild of Mages

Joining the Mages Guild is done by talking to one of the heads of te guild in one of the chapter houses.

If you are expelled from the Mages Guild, you will have to see Trebonius Artorius in Vivec. He will let you back in after 30d. If you wait inside the mages guild each day counts tripple and you can rejoin after only 10d. You can rejoin as often as you like, with the only expense being time.

Guild Guides

The mages offer a service to transport you from one guild hall to another one. Talk to the Guild Guides and select the destination:

  • Erranil in Ald'ruhn
  • Masalinie Merian in Balmora
  • Emelia Duronia in Caldera
  • Flacassia Fauseius in Vivec
  • Iniel in Wolverine Hall

If you have Tamriel Rebuilt installed, there is an additional Guild Guide in Vivec, that will teleport you to the main land.

The mainland Guild Guides work a little differently. They only teleport you to other location in the same 'jurisdiction'.

  • Garrick Usald in Almas Thirr teleports you to Akamora, Andothren or Old Ebonheart
  • Mjara in Akamora teleports you to Almas Thirr, Andothren or Old Ebonheart
  • Ja'Hadra in Andothren teleports you to Akamora, Andothren or Old Ebonheart
  • Soril in Firewatch teleports you to Helnim or Nivalis
  • Thazlorakis in Firewatch teleports you to Old Ebonheart or Vivec
  • Garath Benaque in Helnim teleports you to Firewatch or Nivalis
  • Dagmund Wind-Whisker in Nivalis teleports you to Firewatch or Helnim
  • Elvilde in Old Ebonheart teleports you to Akamora, Almas Thirr or Andothren
  • Barabus Inclodios in Old Ebonheart teleports you to Firewatch or Vivec
