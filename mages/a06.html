Dwemer Tube from Arkngthunch-Sturdumz

Quest GiverEdwinna Elbert in Ald-ruhn
Prerequisite QuestReturn Chimarvamidium
Reward+5 (Mages Guild)

For her research Edwinna Elbert in the Ald-ruhn, Guild of Mages needs a Dwemer Tube. Any tube will do, but she suggests to get it from Arkngthunch-Sturdumz.

The ruins are northwest of Gnisis but you can also get there by traveling to Ald Velothi and following the coast to the west.

Once you have a tube, return it to Edwinna Elbert.
