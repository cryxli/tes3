Meeting with a Wise Woman

Quest GiverSkink-in-Tree's-Shade in Sadrith Mora
Prerequisite QuestVampires of Vvardenfell, Vol II
Reward+5 (Mages Guild), +2 Reputation

Skink-in-Tree's-Shade in the Sadrith Mora, Wolverine Hall: Mage's Guild wants to meet with a Wise Woman of the Ashlander tribes. You are sent to ask around to find someone who would be willing to answer his questions. Skink suggests to ask the Ahemmusa first.

The Ahemmusa Camp is on the far northeast corner of the island, north of Tel Vos. When you talk to the Wise Woman of the camp (Ahemmusa Camp, Wise Woman's Yurt), Sinnammu Mirpal, she refuses to meet with Skink, but suggests her apprentice, Minabibi Assardarainat. She is in the Favel Ancestral Tomb, west of the camp, past the Daedric ruins of Kushtashpi.

When you find Minabibi Assardarainat just inside the tomb, she agrees to meet with Skink but you must first kill the spirit of Kanit Ashurnisammis. Once you get rid of the spirit, Minabibi tells you to return to Sinnammu to get permission for her to meet with Skink.
