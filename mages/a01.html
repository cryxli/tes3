Chronicles of Nchuleft

Quest GiverEdwinna Elbert in Ald-ruhn
Prerequisite Questjoin Guild of Mages
Reward250 gold, +5 (Mages Guild)

Talk with Edwinna Elbert in the Ald-ruhn, Guild of Mages about duties, and you'll find she is looking for a rare Dwemer book Chronicles of Nchuleft. None of the guild halls have a copy, so she gives you 250 septims to go out and buy one, suggesting you try a bookseller.

Possible sellers are Dorisa Darvel in Balmora, Dorisa Darvel: Bookseller and Vasesius Viciulus in Molag Mar, Vasesius Viciulus: Trader. If you have done Sulipund already, you can loot it there. There are other opportunities to steal the book. Or, you can find it in Nchuleft itself.
