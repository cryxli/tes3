Escort Tenyeminwe

Talk with Skink-in-Tree's-Shade in the Sadrith Mora, Wolverine Hall: Mage's Guild about duties. He will ask you to escort the scholar Tenyeminwe from Sadrith Mora, Dirty Muriel's Cornerclub to the ship docks in town.

The quest is very straight foreward. Find Tenyeminwe in the Dirty Muriel's and walk her to the docks.
