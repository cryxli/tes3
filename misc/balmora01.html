Larrius Varro Tells a Little Story

Overhear the latest rumors in Balmora or ask someone about their little secrets to discover that Larrius Varro wishes to speak with you.

Meet Larrius Varro in Moonmoth Legion Fort, Interior, and he suggests that you should take care of the "Bad People" in Balmora. Ask around the Thieves Guild (Balmora, South Wall Cornerclub) about the bad people to get the list of targets:

  • Madrale Thirith
  • Marasa Aren
  • Sovor Trandel
  • Thanelen Velas
  • Vadusa Sathryon.

(The barkeep, Banor Seran, is the only other person in the building.)

Go to the Balmora, Council Club. Kill all the bad people by taunting them first. Then make your way back to Larrius Varro to claim your reward.
