The Corpse and the Skooma Pipe

Overhear the latest rumors in Balmora or ask someone about their little secrets to discover that Larrius Varro wishes to speak with you.

Walking out of Balmora's northern gate, towards Caldera, past the bridge at the intersection of the road to Pelagiad you will soon see a large stone arch on the right just off the trail. Under this arch you can find the body of Ernil Omoran. Search his body to find a note to Tsiya and a skooma pipe, both of which you should take.

Travel back to Balmora and find Tsiya's home (Balmora, Tsiya's House) facing the Odai River on the west side. Tsiya is in her house, and her door is locked (50 points). Once inside, speak to Tsiya about Ernil's note by using the topic "Someone in Particular", and you'll be able to exchange it for 50 gold and 10 samples of Moon Sugar.
