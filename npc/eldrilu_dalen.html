Eldrilu Dalen

Eldrilu Dalen is a Dark Elf priestess who works at the Vos Chapel in Vos.

There she sells books, ingredients, potions, spells, and spellmaking services to all who need them. She is a restocking vendor of the following ingredients:

  • Ash Salts
  • Ash Yam
  • Corkbulb Root
  • Fire Salts
  • Guar Hide
  • Netch Leather
  • Saltrice
  • Scrib Jelly
  • Scrib Jerky
