Alchemy

Governing Attribute: Intelligence

Specialization: Magic

Alchemy allows you to identify magical properties in ingredients and create potions. A higher Alchemy skill allows you to see more effects of ingredients when you hover over them with the cursor. Every 15 levels you see one more effect for up to four.

Eating ingredients and brewing potions trains the skill.

Mages Guild and Tribunal Temple state Alchemy as one of their preferred skills.
