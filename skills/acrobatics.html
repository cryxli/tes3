Acrobatics

Governing Attribute: Strength

Specialization: Stealth

The Acrobatics skill involves jumping, climbing, and avoiding damage from falls. The higher one's Acrobatics, the higher one can jump vertically, the farther one can jump horizontally, and the greater the height one may fall from.

Jumping trains the skill.

Morag Tong and the Thieves Guild list this as one of their favored skills.
